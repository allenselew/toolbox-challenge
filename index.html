Memory Match Game Rules:

  1. Turn over 2 cards at a time.
  2. A player makes a "match" if the two cards turned over are identical.
  3. If the two cards do not match, they are flipped back over.
  4. Matched cards will remain revealed.
  5. The game ends when all cards are matched.
